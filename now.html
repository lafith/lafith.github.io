Now

Updated May 2026

I’m currently based in Abu Dhabi, working as an AI Engineer at MBZUAI’s Agentic Robotics Lab. Most of my work sits around robotics, applied AI, and research systems that need to become reliable tools.

It has been one of the most interesting environments I’ve worked in so far: research-heavy, engineering-heavy, and close enough to hardware that small details matter.

Work

I’m spending most of my time on robotics engineering work that sits between research and real-world use. The day-to-day work is a mix of software integration, perception, system reliability, testing, and making complex robot systems easier to understand and operate.

I’m also working on language-model interpretability, mostly around understanding how model behavior emerges across intermediate layers. That gives me a second thread alongside robotics: one closer to model internals, representation analysis, and reasoning about what a model appears to know before its final output.

Across both threads, I’m interested in making complex systems less opaque, less fragile, and easier to work with.
