Blog / CV / Resume

I am a Senior AI Engineer at the Image-Guided Robotics Group, HTIC–IIT Madras, where we develop medical robotic systems. Our current projects include a Robotic Fetal Ultrasound System and a Spine Surgery Robot. My work focuses on integrating AI-driven computer vision to improve accuracy and efficiency in healthcare.

Previously, I was a Visiting Research Scholar at the University of Alabama at Birmingham, working under Dr. Ritu Aneja. My research focused on deep learning-based analysis of whole slide images from breast cancer patients, leveraging AI to improve pathology insights.

I hold a B.Tech in Biomedical Engineering from NIT Rourkela (2021). My expertise spans:

  • AI-driven image analysis
  • Computational pathology
  • Simulation and Robotics
  • Virtual Reality application
  • Image processing and enhancement

I am passionate about advancing AI and robotics- developing intelligent systems that bridge the gap between cutting-edge research and real-world applications. I welcome connections and collaborations in this exciting field!
